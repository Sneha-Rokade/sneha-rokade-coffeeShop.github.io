Americano Coffee

It is a shot of espresso topped with hot water,it is nutty,earthy flavors.Its like a milky coffee, slightly more subtle in flavor.

Cappuccino Coffee

It is single shot and hot milk with the surface topped with foamed milk and it has a bold coffee taste and sweetness from lactose sugar in milk and top garnish with light dusting of chocolate.

Doppio Coffee

It is a double shot which is extracted using double the amount of ground coffee in a large-sized portafilter basket.

Espresso Coffee

It is a full flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in shots and it has same flavors of coffee but tastes bitter, light sweet, acidic, toasty.

Latte Coffee

It is made of one or two shots of espresso, steamed milk and a final, thin layer of frothed milk on top.

Lungo Coffee

It's is a coffee beverage consisting of one shot of espresso ( a short black) with twice as much hot water forced through the ground coffee.
